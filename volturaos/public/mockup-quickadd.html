My recommendation: Build all three, layered
Voice is the headline feature, but search and recents serve as the safety net. The flow: tap mic → speak → confirm. If voice fails (noise, bad signal), instant search is one tap away. Recents row handles fast repeats. All three live in one "Quick Add" button — nothing changes about the existing estimate builder flow.
